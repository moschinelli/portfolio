I'm Elliot Whittaker — a Liverpool-based video editor and photographer working across commercial, beauty, and travel. I shoot, I film, and I edit, bringing a hands-on approach from concept through to final cut.


My clients include Milani, Rimmel, Flannels, Shiseido, and Ghost — brands that trust me to translate their stories into compelling visuals. Whether it's a high-energy beauty campaign, a product launch, or a travel series, I bring the same obsessive attention to light, motion, and narrative.
